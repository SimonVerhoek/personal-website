People intrigue me. I love working with them in a team, helping them, observing their (sometimes quirky) behaviour.

I dedicated four years of study and over two years of jobs and internships to getting a hold on how you think, why you buy what you buy and what makes you happy as a consumer. I learned about people's perception and decision theories, and practised persuasion and closing deals through promoting and selling a diversity of products and services.
Essentially, all consumers are looking for the same thing: products that acknowledge their needs, understand what they want to do and help them with achieving that goal in an efficient and elegant way.

I enjoy finding people the right product to buy, but still too often I end up with having to choose the least worse one. There is still so much to improve.
Instead of selling people inferior products and feeling guilty, I decided to focus more on designing better products myself. By knowing what people want, how they want it and how to sell it to them, I am sure I can design one hell of a product or experience!
